About me

My name is Vitalii I am from Ukraine. I have been doing web and graphic design for over 2 years . During this time i learned how to creatively and efficiently aproach tasks, as well as analyze the expected results of my design in business.

I have experience in creating logos, labels, printed materials (banners, booklets, brochures, catalogs, business cards, calendars, billboards), company branding, elaboration of corporate identity (a set into a single style).

I am perfectly developing web graphics for Facebook (page design, groups, creating banners for advertising), Instagram (account, posts, instagram advertising), web banners and websites.

Developed one-page websites, online stores, large Internet sites and non-standard business cards and information sites. When working with a task I focus on the details and simplify / optimize the interaction of my design with the consumer / viewer. I bring any task to a high result and full agreement of the client, I work quickly, efficiently and offer my creativity to the client. I treat criticism with understanding.
